Kazakhstan: Facts and Geography

Kazakhstan is a large country of varied terrain in Central Asia.

Here are 5 interesting facts about Kazakhstan:

  1. Kazakhstan has the longest continual border in the world.
  2. Kazakhstan is the largest landlocked country in the world
  3. Kazakhstan is quite ethnically diverse.
  4. The word "Kazakh" translates to "wanderer" or "independent."
  5. Kazakhstan contains one of the largest lakes in the world, "Lake Balkhash."
