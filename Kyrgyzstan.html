Kyrgyzstan Facts and Geography

Kyrgyzstan is a small, mountainous country located in Central Asia.

Here are 5 interesting facts about Kyrgyzstan:

  1. Kyrgyzstan is mostly comprised of mountains.
  2. Kyrgyzstan is completely landlocked.
  3. The nomadic peoples of Kyrgyzstan use mobile dwellings known as "yurts."
  4. Kyrgyzstan was part of the USSR from 1936 to 1990.
  5. Kyrgyzstan gained independence in 1991.
