Uzbekistan Facts and Geography

Kyrgyzstan is a reasonably sized land-locked country located in Central Asia.

Here are 5 interesting facts about Kyrgyzstan:

  1. Alexander the Great once occupied Uzbekistan.
  2. Kyrgyzstan is completely landlocked.
  3. Cotton is known as "White Gold" in Uzbekistan
  4. Uzbekistan is known for its orchards and vineyards
  5. Kyrgyzstan gained independence from the USSR in 1991.
